Finding Your Trading Account Number

Locating your trading account number is simple, follow the below steps:

  • Use the official Exness Login portal to Sign In.
  • In your Personal Area, visit the My Accounts tab.
  • You get your trading account number here beside your balance and account type.

While completing Exness Login to your preferred terminal like the Exness Web Terminal, you will need this number.
